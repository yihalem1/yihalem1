## 🔬 Research Summary

I'm a Master's researcher at UNIST working on **3D human avatar reconstruction**, **3D Gaussian splatting**, and **vision-language reasoning** for hand understanding. My research focuses on creating high-fidelity, animatable digital humans from monocular video with applications in AR/VR, gaming, and digital content creation.

### 🎯 Current Focus
- **Visibility-aware 3D Gaussian avatars** for robust reconstruction under partial body visibility
- **SMPL-X parametric body models** with diffusion-based texture completion
- **Hand pose estimation** and 3D hand-object interaction modeling
